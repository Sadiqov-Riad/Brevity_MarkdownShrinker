# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

Brevity ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our tool handles your data.

## 1. Zero Data Collection
Brevity operates entirely client-side when accessed via our hosted web interface. All Markdown parsing, regex execution, and data sanitization occurs directly in your browser. 
- **We do not collect** the text you paste into the tool.
- **We do not collect** your IP address, browser fingerprint, or personal information.
- **We do not use** tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

## 2. API Usage
If you self-host or use the Brevity FastAPI backend, the text you submit is kept strictly in memory during processing and is immediately discarded. We do not write your data to any persistent storage, database, or log file.

## 3. Open Source Transparency
Because Brevity is 100% open-source, you can inspect the exact code running in your browser or on your server at our GitHub repository. We believe that true data privacy starts with complete transparency.

## 4. Changes to this Policy
We may update this policy periodically as the project evolves. Any changes will be committed directly to this repository.
